Tharsus

Fighting back against covid by helping people return to work

Working with Tharsus – very clever people designing robots and other amazing stuff – we helped create the brand behind new technology for the workplace. In less than two weeks, we went from brief to launch on a product designed to allow people back to work in the height of covid.

But what is it?

Bump is a simple system designed to support social distancing at work. Combining software and hardware, the devices work in real-time to alert wearers whenever they are getting too close to each other. It was instrumental in getting businesses around the world back into the office, and even helped keep runners safe during the 2020 London Marathon.
